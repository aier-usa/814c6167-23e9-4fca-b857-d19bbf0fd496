Encryption is the process of encoding messages in such a way that only authorized parties can access them. Traditionally, encryption has been done through encryption algorithms. These algorithms take plaintext and convert it into ciphertext. These algorithms use keys to encrypt and decrypt messages. In order for a person to decrypt a ciphertext, the person must know what kind of encryption algorithms are uses and the key to decrypt the message. Math encryption refers to encryption technology depending on mathematical algorithms to transformation a plaintext into a ciphertext. Even though a person can get hold of a ciphertext, the person does not understand the meaning of the ciphertext. In theory, a person can crack the ciphertext with the help of a strong math skill and a powerful computer, but the effort is usually too much for ordinary people to engage in such efforts.

Human encryption is just a fancy term for hint. How do you choose a password? You did not choose random combinations of characters because memorizing random characters is a huge burden for human brains. Instead, you choose a password based on an easy-to-remember hint. In this case, a password is a ciphertext and a hint is a plaintext. For example, Hli+19440210 is the password that I have chosen for a website. The hint can be something like: FIRST Initial last PLUS DOB. FIRST Initial represents H. Last is a reference to my last name li. PLUS is a trick (maybe not so tricky) to indicate a + symbol. DOB is another trick. Instead of using my own date of birth, which is 19690331, I have used DOB of my mother who was born on 19440210. As you can tell, there is no way for anyone to convert from "FIRST Initial last PLUS DOB" to "Hli+19440210". No matter how powerful their computers are, corporations and government agencies cannot crack the code because it is not based on mathematical algorithms.

At ihatepassword.com, we encourage you to store "hints" for website, username and password. This is especially true for password storage. Once a hint has been found, you can easily convert it to the real password.
